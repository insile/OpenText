# 词义
- 英：/ˌhedəˈnɪstɪk/； 美：/ˌhedəˈnɪstɪk/
- #词性/adj  享乐主义的
# 例句
- He has always preferred the quiet life , he tells me . It is the media who have played up his hedonistic image .
	- 他对我说自己一直特喜欢恬静的生活，是媒体添油加醋渲染了他享乐主义的形象。
- The cookery course was serious and hedonistic at the same time .
	- 烹饪课既是一件严肃的事又是一种享受。
- A trustafarian knows he or she will never actually run out of money , so adopting a more hedonistic or Bohemian lifestyle for a short period of time would be relatively8 risk-free .
