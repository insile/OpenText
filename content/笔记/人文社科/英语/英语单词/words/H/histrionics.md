- #词性/n  装腔作势；矫揉造作
# 例句
- She was used to her mother 's histrionics .
	- 她习惯了母亲装腔作势的样子。
- When I explained everything to my mum and dad , there were no histrionics .
	- 我一五一十地把一切都向父母解释清楚了。
- There were the usual histrionics when the time came for us to leave .
