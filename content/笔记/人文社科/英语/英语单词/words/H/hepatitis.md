##### 词义
- 英：/ˌhepəˈtaɪtɪs/； 美：/ˌhepəˈtaɪtɪs/
- #词性/n  肝炎
##### 例句
- The doctor tested him for hepatitis .
	- 医生对他进行了肝炎病检查。
- As a major health risk hepatitis is second only to tobacco .
	- 肝炎是仅次于吸烟的主要健康隐患。
- We should require that every student is immunized against hepatitis B
	- 我们应要求每个学生都注射乙肝疫苗。
