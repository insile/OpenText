- 英：/həˈwaɪiː/； 美：/həˈwaɪˌi/

##### 例句
- Prices are often higher in Hawaii than in the continental United States .

- Hawaii remains a popular choice for winter vacation travel .
	- 夏威夷一直是深受人们青睐的冬季假日旅游胜地。
