##### 词义
- 美：/ hæv ə lʊk /；
- #词性/v  看一看
##### 例句
- I insisted that we should have a look at every car.
	- 我坚持我们应该把每辆车都看一看。
- Let's have a look at the TV guide and see what's on.
	- 咱们看看电视节目单上有什么节目。
- I don't suppose (that) I could have a look at your newspaper, could I?
	- 我能不能看看您的报纸？
