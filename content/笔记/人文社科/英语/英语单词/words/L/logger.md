- #词性/n  伐木工
##### 例句
- He was a horse logger , and he used to selectively cut cedar poles from the inland rainforest .
	- 他是一名伐木工，他以前曾在内陆雨林有选择性地砍伐杉木。
- A logger had the drama of falling trees and power saws . I had the drama of getting up to go to the bathroom every few hours .
	- 伐木工拥有伐倒大树和电锯的戏码，我的戏码就是每隔几个钟头上一趟卫生间。
- The loggers say their jobs are faced with extinction because of declining timber sales .
	- 伐木工人说由于木材销量下降，他们的工作恐不复存在。
