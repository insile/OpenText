##### 词义
- 英：/ˈlaɪflaɪk/； 美：/ˈlaɪflaɪk/
- #词性/adj  逼真的；栩栩如生的；生动的
##### 例句
- Evoking lifelike images within the mind .
	- 在脑海中唤起逼真的图像。
- Lifelike three-dimensional characters ; a three-dimensional account of conditions under the new government ; they shot the movie in three-D.
	- 逼真的立体人物；对新政府统治下的情况所做的逼真描述；他们拍摄三维立体电影。
- The painting depicted a hall so lifelike that a casual observer might believe himself to be looking through an open door .
	- 这幅画描绘的厅堂如此逼真，若不细看，还真以为是从一扇开着的门往里看呢。
