# 例句
- He laughed , revealing a line of white teeth .

- The way he laughed was strongly reminiscent of his father .
	- 他笑的样子让人很容易想起他的父亲。
- His first joke got the biggest laugh of the night .
	- 他讲的第一个笑话博得了当晚最开怀的笑声。
