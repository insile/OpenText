##### 词义
- 美：/ wɪˈðaʊt daʊt /；
- #词性/adv  无疑地；确实地
##### 例句
- This meeting has been, without doubt , one of the most useful we have had so far.
	- 这无疑是我们迄今为止最有用的一次会议。
- This was without doubt the most interesting situation that Amanda had ever found herself in.
	- 这无疑是阿曼达遇到过的最有趣的情况。
- Without doubt, Yuan Longping is a real superhero.
	- 毫无疑问，袁隆平是一位真正的超级英雄。
