##### 词义
- 英：/ˌbaɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/； 美：/ˌbaɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/
- #词性/adj  双语的；会说两种语言的；用两种语言(写)的
- #词性/n  能说两种语言的人
##### 例句
- And the answer , she says , is that bilingual people are often better at controlling their attention -- a function called the executive control system .
	- 她说，答案就是，通晓双语的人通常更容易控制他们的注意力——这种能力叫做执行控制系统。
- Earlier and recent psychological studies show that bilingual children are more resourceful and complex intellectually when compared with their monolingual peers .
	- 早年与近年的语言心理研究都显示，学双语的儿童，思想结构比只学单语的儿童复杂。
- She is bilingual in English and Punjabi .
	- 她会说英语和旁遮普语。
