##### 词义
- 英：/ˈʃæsi/； 美：/ˈʃæsi/
- #词性/n  (车辆的)底盘，底座，底架
##### 例句
- Neither the timber frame nor metal chassis were protected against rot.
	- 木构架和金属底盘都没有采取防腐措施。
- He would attach a cable around the chassis of the car and winch it up on to the canal bank
	- 他会把钢丝绳拴在汽车底盘上，用卷扬机把汽车吊到运河岸上。
- Airbus has a futuristic modular concept involving a passenger capsule that can be detached from the road-going chassis and picked up by a helicopter-type machine .
	- 空中客车有一个未来模型概念，它有一个乘客舱，可以从在路上行驶中的底盘上分离出来，并由直升机式的机器接走。
