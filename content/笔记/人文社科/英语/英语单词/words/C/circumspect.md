# 词义
- 英：/ˈsɜːkəmspekt/； 美：/ˈsɜːrkəmspekt/
- #词性/adj  谨慎的；慎重的；考虑周密的
# 例句
- The banks should have been more circumspect in their dealings
	- 那些银行在交易中本该更加谨慎的。
- A Dongfeng victory party in Newport would not , of course , be without its ironies , given the rather more circumspect view taken of Chinese investment in the US compared to Europe .
	- 当然，假如东风在纽波特开庆功会，将不会不具讽刺意味，因为与欧洲相比而言，美国对中国投资持更为谨慎的态度。
- You seem to be implying , in your usual circumspect manner , that perhaps it might not be a wonderful idea .
	- 你好像正以你一贯谨言慎行的方式暗示，那个主意也许不怎么样。
