# 词义
- 英：/kəˈmendəbl/； 美：/kəˈmendəbl/
- #词性/adj  值得赞扬(或嘉许)的
# 例句
- They have laid much too little emphasis on other more socially useful kinds of ethically commendable conduct .
	- 对于其他一些有益于社会，从道德上讲是值得赞扬的品行，却强调得太少了。
- Current efforts to develop alternative energy sources are commendable steps that will decrease pollution and environmental decay .
	- 现行的寻找可替代资源的努力是值得赞扬的，这种做法有利于减少污染，保护环境。
- Considering the restrictions under which she filmed , I think she did a commendable job .
	- 考虑到她拍摄时所受的各种限制，我觉得她交出的答卷值得嘉许。
