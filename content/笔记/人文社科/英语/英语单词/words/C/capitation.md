# 词义
- 英：/ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃn/； 美：/ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃn/
- #词性/n  人头税；按人摊派的费用；按人付费制度
# 例句
- Capitation was related directly to economic development , and influenced directly population fluctuation .
	- 人头税政策不仅直接关系到社会经济的发展，而且也影响到国家人口数量的变化。
- It was just the problem of citizenship and capitation that made Philo take the diplomatic mission to Rome and produce the correlative historical works .
	- 因此，围绕着公民权和人头税的问题，斐洛出使罗马，并创作了相关的“史学作品”。
- As productivity grows , capitation tax is gradually merged into land tax .
	- 随着生产力的提高，人头税逐步并入田亩税中。
