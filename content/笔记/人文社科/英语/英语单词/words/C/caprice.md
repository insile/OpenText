- 英：/kəˈpriːs/； 美：/kəˈpriːs/
- #词性/n  任性；反复无常；善变；(态度或行为的)无缘无故突变

- A hailstorm in July is a caprice of nature .

- The spectre showed a spectre 's ordinary caprice : it gave no sign of being ;
