##### 词义
- #词性/adv  无条件地；绝对地
##### 例句
- He stated categorically that he knew nothing about the deal .
	- 他明确表示他对协议的事一无所知。
- He categorically rejected our offer .
	- 他断然拒绝我们的提议。
- He stated categorically that he was not at the scene of the crime .
	- 他一口咬定当时不在现场。
