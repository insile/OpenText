##### 词义
- 英：/kənˈdʌkʃn/； 美：/kənˈdʌkʃn/
- #词性/n  (热或电等能量的)传导
##### 例句
- Temperature becomes uniform by heat conduction until finally a permanent state is reached .
	- 温度通过热传导变得一致，直到最终达到恒定状态。
- When listening to a recording of your voice , the sound travels through the air and into your ears — what 's referred to as " air conduction " .
	- 当你听语音时，声音会通过空气进入耳朵，这就是所谓的“空气传导”。
- Copper is a good medium for the conduction of heat and electricity .
	- 铜是热和电的良导体。
