##### 词义
- 英：/ˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/； 美：/ˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/
- #词性/adj  可变的；易变的；可能变化的；常变的
##### 例句
- The quality of people is a changeable and dynamic system .
	- 人的素质是一个可变的、动态的状态系统。
- The algorithm can identify the network 's topology automatically with the tree data structure and some mechanisms such as entire initiation after single fault , the best reservation mechanism , intersection between two breaking points , changeable intersection , variable probability and so on .
	- 算法利用树型数据结构自动识别网络拓扑，并采用单一故障完全初始化、最佳保留机制、两断点交叉、可变的交叉及变异概率等机制。
- The weather is very changeable at this time of year .
	- 年年在这个时候天气都变化无常。
