# 例句
- I concur with your opinion on exports .
	- 关于出口问题，我同意你的看法。
- I concur with the speaker in condemning what has been done .
	- 我同意发言者对所做的事加以谴责
- Historians have concurred with each other in this view .
	- 历史学家在这个观点上已取得一致意见。
