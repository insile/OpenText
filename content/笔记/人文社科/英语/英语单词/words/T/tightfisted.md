# 词义

# 例句
- His tightfisted employer was unwilling to give him a raise .

	- 他吝啬的老板不愿意给他提薪。
- The company is tightfisted when it comes to disclosing data or metrics .
	- 这家公司在发布数据或指标时堪称小气。
