- #词性/vt  在…上制榫；用榫接合；（榫般地）牢固接合
##### 例句
- A joint made by inserting tenon on one piece into mortise holes in the other .
	- 通过把一边的凸榫插入另外一边的榫眼造成的接合。
- The retaining wall with anti-sliding tenon met the requirement of anti-sliding stability and , meantime , could improve the bearing capacity of the foundation to some extent due to its embeddability into the foundation .
	- 防滑凸榫挡土墙既能满足抗滑稳定要求，同时由于凸榫对地基的嵌固作用，又能在一定程度上提高地基承载力。
