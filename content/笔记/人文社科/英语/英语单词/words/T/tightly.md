# 词义
- 英：/ˈtaɪtli/； 美：/ˈtaɪtli/
- #词性/adv  紧紧地；紧密地；牢固地
# 例句
- The girl held her father 's hand tightly .
	- 女孩紧紧地拉着她父亲的手。
- She held her little bundle tightly in her arms .
	- 她怀中紧紧地抱着襁褓中的婴儿。
- She closed her eyes tightly in a vain attempt to hold back the tears .
	- 她紧闭双眼，却无法忍住眼泪。
