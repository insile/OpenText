##### 词义
- #词性/adv  在技术上；技术上地
##### 例句
- Many senior managers are technologically illiterate .
	- 许多高级经理都对技术知之甚少。
- Shanghai is a technologically advanced city .
	- 上海是中国的一个技术先进的城市。
- The dialogue is changing from what is technologically possible to what is technologically meaningful , said economist Shawn DuBravac .
	- “话题从什么是技术上可行的转变为什么才是技术上有意义的，”经济学家肖恩·杜布拉瓦茨说道。
