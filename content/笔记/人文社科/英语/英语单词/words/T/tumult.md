##### 词义
- 英：/ˈtjuːmʌlt/； 美：/ˈtuːmʌlt/
- #词性/n  混乱；骚动；骚乱；喧哗；心烦意乱；思绪不宁
##### 例句
- In the increasing tumult questions , answers , and the admonitions of the judge began to overlap .
	- 人群中骚乱声越来越大，质问、辩驳以及法官的频频警告开始乱作一团。
- By the authority of the court , and the exertions of its officers , the tumult among the spectators was at length appeased .
	- 在法庭的威严和官吏们的努力下，观众之间的骚乱终于平息了。
- The country had witnessed scenes of tumult not far removed from civil war
	- 这个国家经历了无异于内战的动乱局势。
