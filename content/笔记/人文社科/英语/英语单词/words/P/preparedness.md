# 词义
- 英：/prɪˈpeərɪdnəs/； 美：/prɪˈperdnəs/
- #词性/n  愿意；准备好的状态
# 例句
- I was surprised by his preparedness to break the law .
	- 我对他打算以身试法感到惊讶。
- The troops are in a state of preparedness .
	- 军队处于备战状态。
- The situation in the capital forced them to maintain military preparedness .
	- 首都的形势迫使他们时刻做好军事准备。
