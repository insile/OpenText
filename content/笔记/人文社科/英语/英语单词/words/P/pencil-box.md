# 例句
- I do not like this pencil-box , please show me another .
	- 我不喜欢这个铅笔盒，请再给我拿一个看看。
- Is that your pencil-box ? Put it away , please .
	- 那是你的铅笔盒吗？请把它收起来。
- Put your pens away into your pencil-box after you use them .
	- 用完以后，把钢笔收拾好放进文具盒里。
