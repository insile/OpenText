##### 例句
- We began to pant before we reached the top of hill .
	- 我们在到达山顶之前已经在喘气了。
- Don 't hurry , don 't pant , breathe in slowly .
	- 不要急，不要直喘气，慢慢地吸气。
- The end of the novel leaves you panting for more .
	- 小说的结尾让人感到意犹未尽。
