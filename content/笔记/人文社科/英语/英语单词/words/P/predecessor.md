- #词性/n  前任；原先的东西；被替代的事物
##### 例句
- The new president reversed many of the policies of his predecessor .

	- 他和他的前任性格不一样。
- The new Prime Minister is an improvement on his predecessor
	- 新上任的总理比他的前任要强。
