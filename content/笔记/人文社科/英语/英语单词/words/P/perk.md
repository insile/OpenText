# 词义
- 英：/pɜːk/； 美：/pɜːrk/

- #词性/adj  同“perky”
# 例句
- The point is that they are a perk and a reward .
	- 关键是，它是额外待遇和奖励。
- The latest and most controversial perk on offer in Silicon Valley a contribution to the cost of freezing female employees ' eggs is a reminder of the career pressures associated with the US tech industry .
	- 硅谷最新提供的、同时也最引争议的额外待遇为女员工报销冷冻卵子费让人看到了美国科技行业的职业压力。
