##### 词义
- 英：/ˈprɒfə(r)/； 美：/ˈprɑːfər/

- #词性/n  贡献；提议；提供物

##### 例句
- His proffer of advice was not accepted finally .
	- 他所提供的建议最后没被采纳。
- Trees and bushes proffer their seeds to the forest animals .
	- 树木为森林里的动物提供种子。
- What advice would you proffer to someone starting up in business ?
	- 您对初入商界的人有何建议？
