# 词义
- 英：/ˈpɒlɪneɪtə/； 美：/ˈpɑləˌneɪtər/
- #词性/n  传粉者；(尤指)传粉昆虫
# 例句
- The interspecies difference in pollinator types mainly depended on whether honeybees were as pollinators .
	- 物种之间的传粉者类群的差异主要在是否有蜜蜂作为访花者。
- The pollinator , on the other hand , wants to remain well-fed with as little effort as possible .
	- 而另一方面，传粉者希望维持在费尽可能少的力气吃饱的状态。
- Flower ecologists have distinguished the wind pollinators , i.e , the anemophilous plants from the zoidogamous plants .
	- 花生态学家把风媒传粉种，即风媒植物，同动物媒植物区别开来。
