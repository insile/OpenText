##### 例句
- He experienced different cultures during his peregrination .
	- 在国外的游历使他体验到不同的文化。
- In the course of this peregrination , each participant visited six of the 90 food stalls in the market .
	- 在游历过程中，每个参与者都参观了这个市场里90个食物摊位中的6个。
