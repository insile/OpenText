##### 词义
- 英：/ˌpriːˈmedɪteɪtɪd/； 美：/ˌpriːˈmedɪteɪtɪd/
- #词性/adj  预谋的；有预谋的；事先策划的
- #词性/v  （尤指犯罪）预谋；预先考虑
##### 例句
- This was clearly a premeditated attempt to silence opposition voices .
	- 这显然是有预谋的压制反对派声音的企图。
- In most states , first-degree murder is defined as an unlawful killing that is both willful and premeditated , meaning that it was committed after planning or " lying in wait " for the victim .
	- 在大多数州，一级谋杀被定义为蓄意且有预谋的非法杀害，即在预谋后实施或“伏击”受害人的犯罪行为。
- The killing had not been premeditated .
	- 这次杀人不是预谋的。
