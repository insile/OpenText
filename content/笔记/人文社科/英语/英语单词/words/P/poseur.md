# 词义
- 英：/pəʊˈzɜː(r)/； 美：/poʊˈzɜːr/
- #词性/n  装腔作势的人；装模作样的人
# 例句
- If anything , our interest is wrapped up in how much money Z - that poseur we remember from university - actually earned in the deal .
	- 要说我们有任何兴趣的话，也不过是Z那个大学来的装腔作势的人究竟从合约中赚了多少钱。
- He had been railed against by them as a prig and a poseur .
