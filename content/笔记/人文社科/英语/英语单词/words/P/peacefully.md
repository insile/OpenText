##### 词义
- 英：/ˈpiːsfəli/； 美：/ˈpiːsfəli/
- #词性/adv  安详地；无痛苦地
##### 例句
- He lived until 1875 and died peacefully at the home of his close friends .
	- 他活到1875年,在他的密友家里安详地死去。
- One week after graduation Rose died peacefully in her sleep .
	- 毕业一星期后，她在梦中安详地离开了人世。
- Both sides committed themselves to settle the dispute peacefully .
	- 双方承诺和平解决争端。
