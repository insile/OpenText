##### 词义
- 英：/rɪˈtrentʃ/； 美：/rɪˈtrentʃ/
- #词性/v  缩减(人员)；节约；紧缩开支；节俭
##### 例句
- Shortly afterwards , cuts in defence spending forced the aerospace industry to retrench .
	- 不久之后，国防开支的削减迫使航空航天业紧缩开支。
- Competition from cheaper imports is forcing manufacturers to retrench .
	- 廉价进口商品的竞争迫使生产者紧缩开支。
- They retrenched by eliminating half the workers .
	- 他们把人员减半以减少支出
