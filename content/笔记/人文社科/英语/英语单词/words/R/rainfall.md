# 词义
- 英：/ˈreɪnfɔːl/； 美：/ˈreɪnfɔːl/
- #词性/n  降雨量；下雨
# 例句
- There has been below average rainfall this month .
	- 这个月的降雨低于平均降雨量。
- Residents were warned not to be extravagant with water , in view of the low rainfall this year .
	- 鉴于今年降雨量少，居民被告诫不得浪费用水。
- There have been four years of below average rainfall .
	- 已经有4年降雨量低于平均值了。
