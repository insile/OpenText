##### 词义

##### 例句
- For some reason we all have to come in early tomorrow .
	- 出于某种原因，我们大家明天都不得不早点来。
- The principal reason for this omission is lack of time .
	- 跳过它的主要原因是时间不足。
- Give me one good reason why I should help you .
	- 我为什么要帮你？给我一个充分的理由。
