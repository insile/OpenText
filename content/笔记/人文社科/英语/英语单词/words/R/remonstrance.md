# 词义
- 英：/rɪˈmɒnstrəns/； 美：/rɪˈmɑːnstrəns/
- #词性/n  抗议；抱怨
# 例句
- He was so much too big for Tellson 's , that old clerks in distant corners looked up with looks of remonstrance , as though he squeezed them against the wall .
	- 他在台尔森银行也是显得太大，连远处角落里的老行员们也都抬起了头，露出抗议的神态，仿佛被他挤到墙边去了。
- There were remonstrances , but he persisted notwithstanding .
	- 虽遭抗议，他仍然坚持下去。
- Mr. Archibald did not give himself the trouble of making many remonstrances .
	- 阿奇博尔德先生似乎不想自找麻烦多方规劝
