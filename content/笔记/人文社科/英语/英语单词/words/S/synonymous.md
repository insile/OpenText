# 词义
- 英：/sɪˈnɒnɪməs/； 美：/sɪˈnɑːnɪməs/
- #词性/adj  同义的；等同于…的
# 例句
- The machines and their products are ubiquitous and are synonymous with plastics for many people .
	- 这类机械和它们的产品普遍存在，对许多人来说与塑料是同义的。
- Happiness is synonymous with confidence , or at least it should be .
	- 开心和自信是同义的，至少是有所关联的。
- Wealth is not necessarily synonymous with happiness .
	- 财富未必等同于幸福。
