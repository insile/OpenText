##### 词义
- 英：/ˈsiːsɪk/； 美：/ˈsiːsɪk/
- #词性/adj  晕船
##### 例句
- It was quite rough at times , and she was seasick .
	- 有时海上波涛汹涌，她就会晕船。
- You will likely feel seasick on your first trip at sea .
	- 初次下海，难免晕船。
- The swaying motion of the ship was making me feel seasick .
	- 船身摇摆不定，让我觉得恶心。
