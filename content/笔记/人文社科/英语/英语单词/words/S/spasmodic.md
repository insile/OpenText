##### 词义
- 英：/spæzˈmɒdɪk/； 美：/spæzˈmɑːdɪk/
- #词性/adj  痉挛(性)的；断断续续的；阵发性的；一阵阵的
##### 例句
- Convulsive motions ; his body made a spasmodic jerk ; spastic movements .
	- 骤发的动作；他的身体痉挛的抽动；痉挛的动作。
- During the time after bronchia scope was put into trachea , the rate of happening of holding breath , irritate and spasmodic trachea in group B was more than group A significantly ( P < 0.05 ) .
	- 支气管镜置入气管后B组屏气、呛咳、气道痉挛的发生率明显高于A组（P<0.05）。
- There was spasmodic fighting in the area yesterday .
	- 昨天，那一地区发生过零星的战斗。
