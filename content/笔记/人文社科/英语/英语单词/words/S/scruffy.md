##### 词义
- 英：/ˈskrʌfi/； 美：/ˈskrʌfi/
- #词性/adj  邋遢的；不整洁的
##### 例句
- His scruffy appearance does not reflect his character .
	- 他邋遢的外表并不反映他的性格。
- Wearing a stained jacket and trousers , he made even my scruffy brother look a model of good grooming .
	- 他穿着沾满污迹的茄克和裤子，甚至使我邋遢的弟弟成了整洁的典范。
- He looked a little scruffy .
	- 他看着有点邋遢。
