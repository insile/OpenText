# 词义
- 英：/spɑ:slɪ/； 美：/ˈspɑrslɪ/
- #词性/adv  稀少；稀疏地；节省地
# 例句
- The ground was sparsely covered with grass .
	- 地面上稀疏地覆盖草丛
- Usually , the Fourier transform and the wavelet transform can not most sparsely represent an image .
	- 通常，傅立叶变换和小波变换不能最稀疏地表示图像。
- The province is heavily forested and sparsely populated .
	- 该省森林茂密，人烟稀少。
