##### 词义
- 英：/ˈsuːtəbli/； 美：/ˈsuːtəbli/
- #词性/adv  适当地；合适地；自然；适宜地；如其所料地
##### 例句
- It is shown that by suitably varying the truncation parameter , the phase singularities reorganize themselves .
	- 结果表明，通过合适地改变截断参数，焦面上位相奇点会重新分布。
- With the aid of the solution matrix of Lax equation satisfied by the eigenfunctions , elliptic variables are introduced suitably , which give a direct relation between the soliton equation and the resulting compatible ordinary differential equations .
	- 借助特征函数所满足Lax方程的解矩阵，合适地引入椭圆变量，由此给出孤子方程与相容的常微分方程之间的直接的关系。
- I am not really suitably dressed for a party .
	- 我穿这样的衣服参加聚会并不十分得体。
