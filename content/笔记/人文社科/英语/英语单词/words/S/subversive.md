# 词义
- 英：/səbˈvɜːsɪv/； 美：/səbˈvɜːrsɪv/
- #词性/adj  颠覆性的；暗中起破坏作用的
- #词性/n  危险分子，颠覆分子
# 例句
- This courageous and subversive movie has attracted widespread critical support .

- There were occasions where breaking tradition was seen as subversive ;
	- 有一些打破传统的场合被视作是颠覆性的；
- He was a known political subversive .
