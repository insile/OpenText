##### 词义
- #词性/v  盯着看
##### 例句
- Don't stare at me – it's impolite!
	- 不要盯着我 - 这很无礼！
- His dazed eyes stare at the eels, which still writhe and entwine.
	- 他茫然地盯着那些仍在扭动缠绕的鳗鱼。
- Strangers would stare at him or even make fun of him.
	- 陌生人会盯着他看，甚至取笑他。
