# 词义
- 英：/ˈsprɪŋtaɪm/； 美：/ˈsprɪŋtaɪm/
- #词性/n  春天；春季；春令
# 例句
- It was springtime and the slopes were ablaze with almond blossoms .
	- 时当春季，怒放的扁桃花使那片山坡鲜红似火
- Springtime usually signals " building time " in the housing world .
	- 通常来讲，春季是住宅建造业大兴土木的时候。
- It was springtime and the fruit trees were in bud .
	- 春天到了，果树发芽了。
