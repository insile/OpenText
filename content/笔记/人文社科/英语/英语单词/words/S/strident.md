# 词义
- 英：/ˈstraɪdnt/； 美：/ˈstraɪdnt/
- #词性/adj  刺耳的；强硬的；咄咄逼人的
# 例句
- In a flurry and strident voices often make others feel nervous .
	- 慌慌张张而又刺耳的声音往往会让别人感到神经紧张。
- At the table on his left the man with the strident voice was still talking remorselessly away .
	- 左边桌上那个声音刺耳的家伙，还在没完没了聒噪个不停。
- Demands for his resignation have become more and more strident .
	- 要求他辞职的呼声越来越强烈。
