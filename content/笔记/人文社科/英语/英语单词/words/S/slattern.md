# 词义
- 英：/ˈslætən/； 美：/ˈslætərn/
- #词性/n  邋遢的女人
- #词性/adj  懒散的；不整洁的
# 例句
- Some of our comrades love to unite long articles with no substance , very much like the foot-bindings of a slattern long as well as smelly .
	- 我们有些同志喜欢写长文章，但是没有内容，真是“懒婆娘的裹脚，又长又臭”。
- But the first and foremost task is to throw the long , smelly foot-bindings of the slattern into the dustbin .
	- 但是主要的和首先的任务，是把那些又长又臭的懒婆娘的裹脚，赶快扔到垃圾桶里去。
- So much had circumstances altered their positions , that he would certainly have struck a stranger as a born and bred gentleman ; and his wife as a thorough little slattern !
	- 环境把他们的地位更换得这么厉害，陌生人乍一看，会认定他是个天生有教养的绅士；而他的妻子则是一个道地的小懒婆！
