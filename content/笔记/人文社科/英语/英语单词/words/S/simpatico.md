##### 词义
- 英：/sɪmˈpætɪkəʊ/； 美：/sɪmˈpætɪkoʊ/
- #词性/adj  志趣相投的；讨人喜欢的；和善的
##### 例句
- He and I are simpatico about a lot of things .
	- 我和他对许多问题看法都很一致。
- where the Italian pastas and his host 's camaraderie were more simpatico .
	- 意大利面和主人的热情招待让他很高兴。
- It 's hard to get an advertising agency and an online agency working simpatico , Mr Eastman says , because traditional agencies tend to see themselves as guardians of the brand , while interactive agencies approach briefs from the consumer 's perspective .
	- 伊斯门表示，很难让广告公司与在线公司和谐共处，一起工作，因为传统广告公司往往视自己为该品牌的捍卫者，而互动式公司则从消费者的角度看待业务。
