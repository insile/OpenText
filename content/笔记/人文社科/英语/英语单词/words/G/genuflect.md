# 词义
- 英：/ˈdʒenjuflekt/； 美：/ˈdʒenjuflekt/
- #词性/vi  (在教堂礼拜时)跪拜，单膝跪拜；卑躬屈膝
# 例句
- He genuflected in front of the altar .
	- 他在祭坛前跪拜。
- They refrained from genuflecting to the laws of political economy .
	- 他们拒不向政治经济学定律顶礼膜拜。
- Of course China was hardly the first country to genuflect in the direction of happiness .
	- 中国当然不是第一个膜拜幸福感的国家。
