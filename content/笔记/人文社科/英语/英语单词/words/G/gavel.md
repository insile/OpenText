##### 例句
- ' Let 's take a ten-minute recess ' , the judge said , pounding his gavel .
	- “现在休庭10分钟。”法官敲了一记木槌说道。
- After that , the sound of the gavel put an end to the unusual session .
	- 在此之后，该木槌声制止不寻常的会议结束。
- It 's rather like using a judge 's gavel to impose order in court .
	- 这种行为很像是法官在法庭上敲击法槌来施行法令。
