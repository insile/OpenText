# 词义
- 英：/əˈpraɪz/； 美：/əˈpraɪz/
- #词性/vt  通知；告知
# 例句
- He came to apprise us that the work had been successfully completed .
	- 他来通知我们工作已胜利完成。
- I need to speak to my professor and apprise him of the change .
	- 我得跟我的教授谈谈，通知他这个变更事项。
- We must apprise them of the dangers that may be involved .
	- 我们必须告诉他们可能涉及的危险。
