##### 词义
- #词性/adv  就某个人而言
##### 例句
- As far as I am concerned, the project is a great success.
	- 就我而言，这个项目是一个巨大的成功。
- As far as she is concerned, the weather is too cold to go out.
	- 在她看来，天气太冷了，不适合出门。
- As far as the students are concerned, the new rules are too strict.
	- 对于学生来说，新规定太严格了。
