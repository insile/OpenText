# 例句
- They rose to applaud the speaker .
	- 他们起立向演讲者鼓掌。
- Every person stood to applaud his unforgettable act of courage .
	- 所有人起立为他不可磨灭的英勇之举鼓掌。
- His efforts to improve the situation are to be applauded .
	- 他为改善状况所作的努力应该受到赞许。
