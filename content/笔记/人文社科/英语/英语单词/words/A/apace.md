# 词义
- 英：/əˈpeɪs/； 美：/əˈpeɪs/
- #词性/adv  迅速地；高速地
# 例句
- The development of complex function theory proceeded apace in the latter part of the nineteenth century .
	- 十九世纪后期，复函数的理论迅速地发展。
- I sat down and saw the box and dice go round apace .
	- 我坐下看骰子在盘子里迅速地转动着。
- The plan is proceeding apace , with another 13 superstores opened in the first half of this year .
	- 该计划进展迅速，在今年上半年又增开了13家大型超市。
