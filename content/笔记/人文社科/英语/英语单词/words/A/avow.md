# 词义
- 英：/əˈvaʊ/； 美：/əˈvaʊ/
- #词性/v  承认；声明；公开宣称
# 例句
- The senator was forced to avow openly that he had received some money from that company .
	- 那个参议员被迫承认曾经收过那家公司的一些钱。
- She was obliged to avow openly that she had been there .
	- 她只得公开承认去过那里。
- An aide avowed that the President had known nothing of the deals .
	- 一位助理声明，总统对这些交易一无所知。
