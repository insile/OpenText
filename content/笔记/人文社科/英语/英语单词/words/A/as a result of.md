##### 词义
- #词性/prep  作为...的结果，由于
##### 例句
- She died as a result of her injuries.
	- 她由于受伤而死亡。
- Their bodies had suffered contortion as a result of malnutrition.
	- 由于营养不良他们的躯体都变了形。
- Millions will face starvation next year as a result of the drought.
	- 由于发生旱灾，明年将有数百万人面临饥饿的威胁。
