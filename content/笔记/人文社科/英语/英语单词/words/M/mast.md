# 词义
- 英：/mɑːst/； 美：/mæst/
- #词性/n  桅杆；船桅；旗杆；天线塔
- #词性/vt  在…上装桅杆
# 例句
- The main cabin is situated forward of the mast .
	- 主舱在桅杆的前面。
- The collective name for mast , boom and sails on a boat is the ' rig ' .
	- 船的桅杆、帆桁和帆总称为“索具”。
- Flags were flying at half mast on all public buildings .
	- 所有的公共建筑都降半旗。
