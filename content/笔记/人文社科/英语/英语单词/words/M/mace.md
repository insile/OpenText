- 英：/meɪs/； 美：/meɪs/
- #词性/n  狼牙棒(古代兵器)；权杖；肉豆蔻干皮，肉豆蔻种衣(烹调香料)

- An official who carries a mace of office . The Minister of State commands the services of many officials .
