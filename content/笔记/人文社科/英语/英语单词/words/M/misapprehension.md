##### 词义
- 英：/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhenʃn/； 美：/ˌmɪsæprɪˈhenʃn/
- #词性/n  误解；误会
##### 例句
- She could not attribute such behaviour to mistake or misapprehension of any kind .
	- 她也不能把这种行为归之任何的误会或误解。
- I thought you wanted to see me but I was clearly under a complete misapprehension .
	- 我以为你想见我，显然完全是我误解了。
- I was under the misapprehension that the course was for complete beginners .
	- 我误以为这门课是给纯粹的初学者开的。
