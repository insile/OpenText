# 词义
- 英：/ˈfɪtfl/； 美：/ˈfɪtfl/
- #词性/adj  断断续续的；间歇的；一阵阵的
# 例句
- In fact , some mothers mistake their newborn puppies for rodents because of their fitful movements and squealing .
	- 事实上，一些狗妈妈由于小狗断断续续的运动和号叫而将其误认为老鼠。
- Such sleep as she got was naturally fitful ; she fancied she heard strange noises , but persuaded herself that they were caused by the breeze .
	- 她这样睡觉自然是断断续续的；她总觉得听见了奇怪的声音，但是她又劝自己说，那些声音只不过是由风引起的。
- The government is making slow and fitful progress in these negotiations .
	- 政府从这些谈判中正取得缓慢的、间断性的进展。
