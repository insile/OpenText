##### 词义
- 英：/ˈfækʃn/； 美：/ˈfækʃn/

##### 例句
- Government leaders mostly come from a single faction in the civil war .
	- 政权领导者大多来自于内战的一个派别。
- Arguments break out between different faction at the party conference .
	- 在党的会议上不同派别之间发生了争论。
- A rebel faction has split away from the main group .
	- 一帮反叛者从核心组织中分裂了出去。
