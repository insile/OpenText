# 词义
- 英：/ˈfɜːmnəs/； 美：/ˈfɜrmnəs/
- #词性/n  硬度；坚固，坚牢，坚定
# 例句
- Well buildings have three conditions : commodity , firmness and delight .
	- 良好的建筑有三个条件：方便，坚固和愉悦。
- The utility model also has the advantages of firmness , durability and comfort .
	- 本实用新型还具有坚固、耐用、舒适的优点。
- He was a man of intelligence and of firmness of will .
	- 他是个聪明而又意志坚定的人。
