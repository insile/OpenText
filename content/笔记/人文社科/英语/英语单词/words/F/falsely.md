# 词义
- 英：/ˈfɔːlsli/； 美：/ˈfɔlsli/
- #词性/adv  错误地；虚假地；欺诈地
# 例句
- She smiled falsely at his joke .
	- 她听了他的笑话假装笑了。
- So far from admitting his own mistake , he falsely accused his critic .
	- 他非但不认错，还倒打一耙。
- Somebody has falsely claimed the watch .
	- 有人冒领了那只手表。
