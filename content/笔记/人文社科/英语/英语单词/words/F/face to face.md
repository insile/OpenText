##### 词义
- 英：/ ˌfeɪs tə ˈfeɪs /；美：/ ˌfeɪs tə ˈfeɪs /；
- #词性/adv  面对面
##### 例句
- Her eyes flicked from face to face .
	- 她的眼光扫过人们的脸。
- She has suddenly come face to face with her own mortality.
	- 突然，她已经与死神面对面了。
- The two have never met face to face before.
	- 两个人过去从未见过面。
