##### 词义
- 美：/ frəm taɪm tu taɪm /；
- #词性/adv  时不时地；有时候
##### 例句
- From time to time he passed on confidential information to him.
	- 他不时地传送机密情报给他。
- Her daughters visited him from time to time when he was bedridden.
	- 他卧床不起时她的女儿们不时地来探望他。
- The baby cries from time to time .
	- 这个宝宝时不时地哭。
