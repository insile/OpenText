# 词义
- 英：/ˈvɪgərəsli/； 美：/ˈvɪgərəsli/
- #词性/adv  精神旺盛地；精神旺盛地，活泼地
# 例句
- Furthermore , we should reform the contents of classroom teaching and improve teaching methods so that students may learn actively and vigorously .
	- 必须改革课堂教学结构，改进教法，让学生主动、活泼地学；
- The party campaigned vigorously in the north of the country .
	- 该党在本国北部展开了强有力的竞选运动。
- They had campaigned vigorously for unilateral nuclear disarmament .
	- 他们曾致力于单方面裁减核武器运动。
