##### 词义
- 英：/ˈəʊdiəm/； 美：/ˈoʊdiəm/
- #词性/n  厌恶；憎恶；公愤
##### 例句
- Behaviour that exposed him to odium
	- 他那招人憎恶的行为
- He incurred the odium of everyone by sacking the old caretaker .
	- 他因解雇老管理员而激起公愤。
- The complainant has been exposed to public odium , scandal and contempt .
	- 原告遭到了公众的憎恨、非议与蔑视。
