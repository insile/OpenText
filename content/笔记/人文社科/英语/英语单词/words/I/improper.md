# 词义
- 英：/ɪmˈprɒpə(r)/； 美：/ɪmˈprɑːpər/
- #词性/adj  不正确的；不正当的；不适当的；错误的；不合适的；不道德的；不得体的；不诚实的
# 例句
- The tender inviting party decides in advance internally the successful tenderer , and adopts improper criteria when making the decision on the successful tenderer ;
	- 招标者预先内定中标者，在确定中标者时采用不正当的做法决定取舍；
- Management and employees , typically low-paid by both absolute and international standards , would engage in improper transactions through which their own benefit was clearly greater than that of the enterprise .
	- 管理层和员工（按绝对和国际标准衡量通常薪酬均很低）会从事不正当的交易，在这些交易中他们显然会将个人利益置于企业利益之上。
- The improper use of medicine could lead to severe adverse reactions .
	- 用药不当会产生严重的不良反应。
