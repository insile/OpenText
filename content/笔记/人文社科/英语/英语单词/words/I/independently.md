##### 词义
- 英：/ˌɪndɪ'pendəntlɪ/； 美：/ˌɪndɪˈpɛndəntlɪ/
- #词性/adv  独立地；自立地；无关地
##### 例句
- Cross-overs have occured independently of each other between pair of homologous chromosomes .
	- 交换现象独立地在每一对同源染色体之间发生。
- Not surprisingly , the U.S.runs its policy independently , while China adjusts its own accordingly .
	- 因此很自然的，美国独立地施展政策，而中国相应地调整其自身。
- It was the first time that she had lived independently .
	- 那是她第一次独立生活。
