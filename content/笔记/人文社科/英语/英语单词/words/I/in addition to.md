##### 词义
- 英：/ɪn əˈdɪʃn tu/； 美：/ɪn əˈdɪʃn tu/
- #词性/prep  除了……之外：表示在已经提到的事物之外，还有额外的事物或观点
##### 例句
- In addition to surgery and radiation therapy , patients receiving adjuvant systemic therapy ( hormone therapy or chemotherapy ) had more survival benefit than those who did not .
	- 另外除了手术及放射治疗，接受辅助性全身性治疗（激素治疗或化学治疗）的病人较未接受者有存活上的明显优势。
- In addition to assessment on project executing processes and effectiveness , the evaluation index system should also be able to evaluate the vertical and horizontal extensibility of the informationization development in detail .
	- 另外除了项目实施过程和效果评价外，评价指标体系还要能够体现对信息化发展内容的纵向和横向可扩展性进行考核。
- In addition to these arrangements , extra ambulances will be on duty until midnight .
	- 除了这些安排以外，另增救护车值班至午夜。
