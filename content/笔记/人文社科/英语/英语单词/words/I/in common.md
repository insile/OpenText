##### 词义
- #词性/adv  共同的；共有的
##### 例句
- We had a surprising amount in common .
	- 我们共同之处出奇地多。
- We happened to discover we had a friend in common .
	- 我们碰巧发现我们有个共同的朋友。
- Plants are grouped into botanical "families" that have certain characteristics in common .
	- 具有某些共同特点的植物被归为植物学的科。
