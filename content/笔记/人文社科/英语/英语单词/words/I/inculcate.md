##### 词义
- 英：/ˈɪnkʌlkeɪt/； 美：/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/
- #词性/vt  反复灌输；谆谆教诲
##### 例句
- Great care was taken to inculcate the values of nationhood and family .
	- 花费了很多心力反复灌输国家与家庭观念。
- At the same time , it will be my aim to inculcate by my official acts the necessity of exercising by the General Government those powers only that are clearly delegated ;
	- 同时，我的目标是，以我的官方行动，反复灌输中央政府只行使明确地授予它的权力的必要性；
- The aim is to inculcate business people with an appreciation of different cultures
	- 其目的是教导商界管理人员逐渐学会欣赏不同的文化。
