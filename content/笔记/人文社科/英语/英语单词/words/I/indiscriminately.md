# 词义
- #词性/adv  不加区别地；随意地；不加选择地
# 例句
- The soldiers fired indiscriminately into the crowd .
	- 士兵对着人群胡乱开枪。
- One should not stick labels indiscriminately on others .
	- 不能乱扣帽子。
- We should not use proverbs and allusions indiscriminately .
	- 不要滥用成语典故。
