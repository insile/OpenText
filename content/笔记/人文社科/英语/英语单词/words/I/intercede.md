##### 词义
- 英：/ˌɪntəˈsiːd/； 美：/ˌɪntərˈsiːd/
- #词性/vi  调解；(为某人)说情；(向某人)求情
##### 例句
- Also common to the prophets is their ability to intercede for their people .
	- 还共同先知是他们的调解能力为自己的人民。
- Farm and labor movements began to ask the government to intercede on their behalf .
	- 农民和劳工运动开始要求政府代表他们出面调解。
- They interceded with the authorities on behalf of the detainees .
	- 他们为被拘留者向当局求情。
