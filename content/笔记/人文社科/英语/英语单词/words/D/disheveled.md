##### 词义
- #词性/adj  凌乱的；凌乱的，不整洁的
##### 例句
- He arrived on January 9 , disheveled and much the worse for wear .
	- 他是1月9号到的，蓬头垢面，疲惫不堪。
- His hair was disheveled , and his face was unshaven and gray .
	- 他头发凌乱，没刮胡子，脸色苍白。
- They were a little disheveled , but still determined to be there .
	- 虽然都有点衣冠不整，但仍决定在那里。
