##### 词义
- 英：/ˈdeməʊ/； 美：/ˈdemoʊ/
- #词性/n  录音样带；试样唱片
- #词性/v  试用（尤指软件）；演示；示范
##### 例句
- During this time , she sang for her brother 's band and recorded her first demo in a studio her friend worked for .

- He listened to one of my demo tapes
	- 他听了我的一盘录音样带。
- I 'll give you a demo .
	- 我来给你作个示范。
