##### 例句
- Someone who does not believe the doctrine of free will .
	- 不信奉自由意志教条的人。
- And that may not be within church doctrine .
	- 那可能在教堂的教条之外。
- The doctrine was based on three fundamental principles .
	- 这个学说建立在三条基本原理之上。
