##### 词义

- #词性/vt  支付，付给(已开支的款项)
##### 例句
- Many countries helped defray the building costs .
	- 许多国家协助支付建筑的花费。
- It was not enough to defray the extra cost .
	- 这笔钱不够支付额外的费用。
- The government has committed billions toward defraying the costs of the war .
	- 政府已承诺拨款数十亿支付战争的开支。
