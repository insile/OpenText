##### 词义
- 英：/dɜːθ/； 美：/dɜːrθ/
- #词性/n  缺乏；不足
##### 例句
- There was a dearth of reliable information on the subject .
	- 关于这个课题缺乏可靠资料。
- A dearth of technical talent seems to be another widespread problem .
	- 缺乏技术人才则是这个行业的另一个普遍问题。
- Many people in that country died because of dearth of food .
	- 那个国家有许多人因为缺少粮食而死。
