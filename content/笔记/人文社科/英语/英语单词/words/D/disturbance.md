##### 词义
- 英：/dɪˈstɜːbəns/； 美：/dɪˈstɜːrbəns/

##### 例句
- He was charged with causing a disturbance after the game .

- During the disturbance which followed , three Englishmen were hurt .
	- 在接下来的骚乱中，3名英国人受伤。

	- 建筑施工不断制造噪音、灰尘和干扰。
