# 词义
- 英：/ʌnˈlʌki/； 美：/ʌnˈlʌki/
- #词性/adj  不幸的；倒霉的；不吉利的；晦气的；不顺利的
# 例句
- However , I bless those unlucky people and hope the situation will be better , and I want to appreciate my dear motherland and parents who give me the colorful life .

	- 从一开始，我们就是个很不幸的国家。
