##### 词义
- #词性/n  城市化；城镇化；都市化；城市化过程（等于 urbanization）
##### 例句
- With urbanisation the antagonism between rich and poor sharpened
	- 伴随着城市化的推进，贫富间的对立情绪也加剧了。
- China is still in the early stages of urbanisation .
	- 中国仍处在城市化初期阶段。
- Urbanisation also offers huge potential for long-term domestic demand .
	- 城镇化为长期扩大内需提供巨大潜力。
