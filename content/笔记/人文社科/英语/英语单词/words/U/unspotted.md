# 词义
- #词性/adj  没有斑点（瑕庇）的；纯洁的；无污渍
# 例句
- One felt that he had kept himself unspotted from the world .
	- 旁人会感到他没被世俗污染。
- The heart must be kept pure and calm as an unspotted mirror .
	- 内心必须保持明镜一般的纯洁与安宁。
- He was able to get in the building unspotted and unsuspected .
	- 他未被察觉、不被怀疑地进了大楼。
