# 词义
- 英：/ˈevɪdəntli/； 美：/ˈevɪdəntli/
- #词性/adv  显然；明显地；据说
# 例句
- She walked slowly down the road , evidently in pain .
	- 她沿路慢慢地走着，显然很痛苦。
- Quite evidently , it has nothing to do with social background .
	- 很显然这与社会背景没有任何关系。
- Evidently , she had nothing to do with the whole affair .
	- 据说，她与整件事情毫无关系。
