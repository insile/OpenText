##### 词义

- #词性/v  确保；保证；担保
##### 例句
- Ensure there is a free flow of air around the machine .
	- 要确保机器周围空气畅通。
- Use a black pen on white paper to ensure good reproduction .
	- 用白纸黑笔，以确保复印清晰。
- They could not ensure a smooth transfer of political power .
	- 他们无法保证政权的顺利交接。
