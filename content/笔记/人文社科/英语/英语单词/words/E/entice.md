##### 词义
- 英：/ɪnˈtaɪs/； 美：/ɪnˈtaɪs/
- #词性/vt  引诱；诱使
##### 例句
- Try and entice the child to eat by offering small portions of their favourite food .
	- 给孩子少许爱吃的食物，设法诱使他们吃饭。
- Advertisements are designed to entice people into spending money ( to spend money ) .
	- 广告宣传的目的是诱使人花钱。
- The bargain prices are expected to entice customers away from other stores .
	- 低廉的价格意在把顾客从其他商店吸引过来。
