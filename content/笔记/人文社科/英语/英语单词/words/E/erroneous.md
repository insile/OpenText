# 词义
- 英：/ɪˈrəʊniəs/； 美：/ɪˈroʊniəs/
- #词性/adj  错误的
# 例句
- They have arrived at some erroneous conclusions .
	- 他们得出了一些错误的结论。
- He has an erroneous opinion about the problem .
	- 他对那个问题有错误的看法。
- Some people have the erroneous notion that one can contract AIDS by giving blood
	- 一些人错误地认为献血会让人感染艾滋病。
