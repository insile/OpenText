##### 词义
- 英：/ɪksˈtɛnsɪvli/； 美：/ɪkˈstɛnsɪvli/
- #词性/adv  广泛地；广大地
##### 例句
- She has travelled extensively .
	- 她游历甚广。
- He had worked extensively on the anatomy of living animals .
	- 他在活体解剖方面经验丰富。
- Latterly , he has written extensively about alternative medicine .
	- 近来，他写了大量有关替代医学的文章。
