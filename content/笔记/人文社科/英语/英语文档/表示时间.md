##### 表示时间
- 表示时间
	- 整点时间
	    - 2:00: two o'clock
	    - 5:00: five o'clock
	- 小时和分钟
	    - 3:15: three fifteen 或 a quarter past three
	    - 4:30: four thirty 或 half past four
	    - 7:45: seven forty-five 或 a quarter to eight
	- 上午/下午
	    - AM（上午）: 10:00 AM: ten o'clock in the morning
	    - PM（下午）: 6:00 PM: six o'clock in the evening
