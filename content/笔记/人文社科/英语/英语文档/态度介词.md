##### 态度
- [[in favour of]]（支持）
	- The majority voted in favour of the proposal.
	- Those present were in favour of change.
- [[on behalf of]]（代表）
	- I’m speaking on behalf of the entire team.
	- On be half of the department I would like to thank you all.
- [[pro]]（支持）
	- She is pro free education for all.
	- He has always been pro the environment.
- [[against]]（反对）
	- We're playing against the league champions next week.
	- That's against the law.
- [[anti]]（反对）
	- He is anti everything the new policy represents.
- [[versus]]（对立）
	- The match is Barcelona versus Real Madrid.
	- It's a classic case of good versus evil.
