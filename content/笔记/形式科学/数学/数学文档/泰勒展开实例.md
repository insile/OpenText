##### 泰勒展开实例
- 泰勒展开实例
	- $\displaystyle e^x=\sum^{\infty}_{n=0}\frac{x^n}{n!}$, $(-\infty,+\infty)$
		- $\displaystyle 1+x+\frac{x^2}{2!}+...+\frac{x^n}{n!}+...$
	- $\displaystyle \frac{1}{1+x} = \sum^{\infty}_{n=0} (-1)^nx^n$, $(-1,1)$
		- $1-x+x^2+...+(-1)^nx^n+...$
	- $\displaystyle \frac{1}{1-x} = \sum^{\infty}_{n=0} x^n$, $(-1,1)$
		- $1+x+x^2+...+x^n+...$
	- $\displaystyle \ln{(1+x)} = \sum^{\infty}_{n=1} (-1)^{n-1}\frac{x^n}{n}$, $(-1,1]$
		- $\displaystyle x-\frac{x^2}{2}+\frac{x^3}{3}+...+(-1)^{n-1}\frac{x^n}{n}+...$
	- $\displaystyle \sin{x} = \sum^{\infty}_{n=0} (-1)^n\frac{x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!}$, $(-\infty,+\infty)$
		- $\displaystyle x-\frac{x^3}{3!}+\frac{x^5}{5!}+...+(-1)^n\frac{x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!}$
	- $\displaystyle \cos{x} = \sum^{\infty}_{n=0} (-1)^n\frac{x^{2n}}{(2n)!}$, $(-\infty,+\infty)$
		- $\displaystyle x-\frac{x^2}{2!}+\frac{x^4}{4!}+...+(-1)^n\frac{x^{2n}}{(2n)!}$
