##### 符合导数定义的极限
- $\displaystyle\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{e^h-1}{h}$
	- $\displaystyle f(x)=e^x$
	- $\displaystyle f'(x)=\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{e^{x+h}-e^x}{h}=e^x$
	- $\displaystyle\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{e^{0+h}-e^0}{h}=e^0=1$
- $\displaystyle\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{\ln{(1+h)}}{h}$
	- $f(x)=\ln{x}$
	- $\displaystyle f'(x)=\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{\ln{(x+h)}-\ln(x)}{h}=\frac{1}{x}$
	- $\displaystyle\lim_{h\rightarrow0}\frac{\ln{(1+h)}-\ln(1)}{h}=1$
