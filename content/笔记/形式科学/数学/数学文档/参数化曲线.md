##### 参数化曲线
- 参数化曲线
	- 由[[解析几何]]可得一些使用[[函数|向量函数]]或[[参数方程]]表示的参数化曲线
		- $\pmb{r}(t) =  f(t)\pmb{i}+g(t)\pmb{j}+ h(t)\pmb{k}$ , $\left\{\begin{matrix} x=f(t) \\ y=g(t) \\ z=h(t)\end{matrix}\right.$ , $t\in\mathbb{R}$
		- ![[Pasted image 20240726083949.png]]
- 参数化曲线几何量
	- [[切向量]], [[法向量]], [[弧长]], [[曲率]], [[挠率]]
	- 螺旋线
		- $\pmb{r}(t) =  \sin(t)\pmb{i}+\cos(t)\pmb{j}+ t\pmb{k}$ , $0\leq t\leq\pi$
			- $\pmb{r}(\pi) =  (0,-1,\pi)$
		- $\pmb{r}'(t) =  \cos(t)\pmb{i}-\sin(t)\pmb{j}+ \pmb{k}$ 
			- $\pmb{r}'(\pi) =  (-1,0,1)$
		- $\pmb{r}''(t) =  -\sin(t)\pmb{i}-\cos(t)\pmb{j}$ 
			- $\pmb{r}''(\pi) =  (0,1,0)$
		- $\displaystyle s =\int^{\pi}_{0}\sqrt{2}{\rm d}t=\sqrt{2}\pi$
		- $\displaystyle\kappa=\frac{||\pmb{T}'(t)||}{||\pmb{r}'(t)||}=\frac{1}{2}$
